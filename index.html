A clear, welcoming route for landlords working with [Council / Authority Name].

Integral Lettings helps landlords understand the process of making a property available for council-housed tenants, with calm guidance, practical communication, and support from first enquiry to ongoing contact.

This is not about pressure or selling. It is about helping landlords explore a structured option where they can provide housing and receive compensation through a supported arrangement.
